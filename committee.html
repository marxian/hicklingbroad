Club Committee 2011-2012

The club committee are here to help you both on and off the water. The committee is made up of club members who give their time voluntarily to ensure our club runs smoothly. If you need to contact anybody on the committee then email us at enquiries@hicklingbroad.com. In addition to the committee members listed below, there are many unsung heroes who give many hours of their time to help the club directly and indirectly throughout the year. If you would like to become involved in the running of the club, we would love to hear from you via the email address above.
