For Sale

Goldeneye

Goldeneye was built originally in 1928 and extensively rebuilt by Paul Bown about seven years ago. Much of her structure is new and she is in excellent condition.

She is 20 feet long and has an aluminium mast with a nearly new sail from Mike McNamara. The jib, from Jeckells is also nearly new and was made to fit the boat. Fittings are recent and of good quality. The rudder was made by a specialist. There is a launching trolley and also a (rather old) cover.

There is a measurement certificate and the boat has been allocated a handicap by the Owners Association.

I no longer have anywhere to store this boat so she must go. Any offer considered.

If you'd like more detail, ring Robin on 01692 598296.

Wayfarer 448

The hull is in need of varnish and repainting, but, is otherwise sound.

Solo 3897 - Grey Matter
Price £2,250

Composite Solo well known at Hickling Broad SC. Built by Severn Sailboats in 1993. Mast Proctor C sleeved (I think!). Comes with reasonable North sail + another. On old West Mersea launching trolley with new (2010) road part of combi. Includes somewhat worn boom down cover. Competitive - when not sailed by me. Since I bought her from Kevin in autumn 2009 I have had her renovated by Severn, including topsides spray varnished, and added a new road part of the West Mersea combi trailer. Previously owned by Tom Clancy and Kevin Postlethwaite - amongst others
