All comers races are restricted to monohull yachts without cabins. Prizes for all points series will be based on the results using the RRS low point scoring system. 6 races over 2 days with 5 to count. Prizes will be awarded immediately after racing on the Sunday. In the event of a tie, the result will be decided by the 'most firsts' followed if neccessary by the most second places etc.

Entry Fee £15 For The Weekend (Helms Under 18 Free)
