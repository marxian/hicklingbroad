More details:
Four races - Three to count

Appendix P, Special procedures for Rule 42 (Yellow Flag System) will apply.
 
There will be 4 individual racing fleets with separate starts: Fast and Slow handicap, and 2
starts for classes of sufficient numbers, which could be Topper, Lasers or Optimists
Classes are to be confirmed on the day dependant on entries
 
Should there be sufficient number of Laser entries to warrant a class start, ALL rig sizes will compete
in the same series with the finishing order determined by taking account of respective PNs.

* Starting Flag to be confirmed at the briefing.

The Regatta will be governed by the Racing Rules of Sailing, in addition by Hickling Broad Sailing Club's
Sailing Instructions and by the Supplementary Sailing Instructions

Lunches and refreshments will be available from the Galley all day
