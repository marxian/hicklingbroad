Webmaster's Page

Thank for you for visiting the Hickling Broad Sailing Club website.

Linking to this website

If you are from another sailing club that would like to create a reciprocal link with Hickling Broad Sailing Club - please email your details and club/organisation URL so we can visit the site and evaluate its suitability for our links page. Submissions should be made to: enquiries@hicklingbroad.com.

Please note that we do not allow any commercial links on the links page. All links are added at the discretion of the club committee. All links, logos and images that appear on our 'Useful Links' page are published with permission of those clubs/organisaitons.

If you currently have a link with Hickling Broad Sailing Club and require an authorised image for use on your site - please use the following .GIF image, feel free to alter the size proportionally to suit your requirements:

Right click on the image above and select 'Save Picture As' (or 'save image as' in Firefox) to download your copy. The above image is 90x60 pixels and 3k. If you require artwork for printing or higher resolution graphics - please email us at the address above.

Technical Problems

If you are experiencing any technical problems with our website, such as:

> broken links
> graphics not loading
> 404 'page not found' errors

Please report these to our webmaster by email at the address below. Our webmaster can be contacted at: webmaster@hicklingbroad.com

Privacy Policy

No personal information about you is recorded or stored. Any data supplied in forms or emails will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, rented or given to any third party organisations.
