Club Boat List

HBSC has a number of club boats available for use by club members by prior arrangement through the booking system. Club boats cannot be used by temporary members or guests except that they may crew a double hander helmed by a club member. All members using a club boat must book in advance using the forms in Club Boat Log Book which is retained in the club house . Members must complete a Club Boat Booking Form.

Please note that the use of both RS Feva's and the Race Topper is restricted.
