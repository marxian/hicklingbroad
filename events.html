Womack Cruise - 23rd June

Again this year we intend to cruise to Hunters Boatyard at Womack and stay the night.
Many of you have been before so know the format but for those who are curious the basic drill is as follows. You can sleep on your boat or bring a small (ish) tent, showers and toilets are available at the boatyard.
I have booked a large table(s) at the Kings Arms in Ludham for 7 p m, which is a 15min walk from Womack, for evening meal.
Leaving the Club on saturday 23rd June at approx 11 a m and returning to Hickling on the Sunday. Any boat and crews of all ages welcome, it is not a race so although we try and leave together it does not matter if you join us at any part of the weekend.
We gather at Potter Heigham Bridge for something to eat, chip shop available, and help can be given if getting mast down etc is too much for your crew alone.
No need to commit to coming but it would be useful if you let me know if you are. The only commitment I need is to know what your meal requirements are for Saturday night, I need to order each meal by Saturday lunch as the are so many of us. I normally get your order on Saturday before we leave the Club, but if you are not leaving with the rest of us perhaps you could let me know what you would like. The full menu can be seen on the Kings Arms website : kingsarmsludham.co.uk
If you want to know any more details please contact me on 01603 737213, 07836748433
Richard Hetherington
p s I will be away until wednesday 20th June so leave any contact till then please.
