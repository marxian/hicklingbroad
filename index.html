2011 Photo Competition Entries

  • We are a thriving friendly club with a proud tradition of promoting dinghy sailing at all levels.

  • We have a policy of encouraging our youth members. We were proud to enter a team of 30 in the Broadland Youth Regatta. Some of our juniors joined as complete beginners and have now progressed to compete in national competitions.

  • We're one of the leading sailing clubs in Norfolk, with many dinghy classes including: Wayfarer, Laser, Topper, Solo, Punt, RS Tera & Feva, Splash, 420, Phantom, GP14, RS400, Comet and Bittern classes.

  • Hickling is the largest, most scenic and ecologically important
    of the Norfolk Broads.

  • The club is a member of the Royal Yachting Association (RYA) and is affiliated to the Norfolk and Suffolk Boating Association.
